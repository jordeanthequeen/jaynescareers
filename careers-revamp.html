Who We Are

We’re builders — of structures, of relationships, of careers.

And we’re owners. Every employee owns stock in the company and has a financial stake in its success. We promote from within, and anyone can grow into leadership. Some of our current leaders started as laborers. When we have good people, we keep them, help them grow, and empower them to guide the next generation toward excellence.

Ownership affects how we approach the work, too. It means doing the job right, even if the right way is harder. It means having high standards and walking away from opportunities where those standards can’t be met. It means standing behind the work you do and accepting responsibility for its success or failure. And it means never cutting corners — not on the work we do, and not in the way we take care of our employees.

Jaynes is Your Way Up

At Jaynes, ownership isn’t just a principle — it’s a promise. Through our Employee Stock Ownership Plan (ESOP), every team member holds a stake in the company’s success. But the ESOP is more than just a financial benefit — it’s a symbol of trust, responsibility, and shared purpose. It's one of the many ways we invest in the people who help us raise the bar on excellence.

That investment extends far beyond the job site. We prioritize your well-being and peace of mind, which is why we haven’t raised employee health insurance premiums in over a decade. Our benefits include comprehensive life and disability coverage — and access to an on-site health clinic dedicated exclusively to Jaynes employees and their families.
